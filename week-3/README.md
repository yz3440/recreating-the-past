## Artists

**Muriel Cooper** (1925-1994)

American designer and educator, founding director of MIT Media Lab's Visible Language Workshop. Pioneer in digital typography and information design.

**John Maeda** (b. 1966)

American designer and technologist, former president of RISD. Known for computational approaches to graphic design and his work at MIT Media Lab.
