## Topic

Pattern

## Artists

**Anni Albers** (1899-1994)

German-American textile artist and printmaker, influential figure at the Bauhaus. Known for her systematic exploration of weaving patterns and textile design.

**Odili Donald Odita** (b. 1966)

Nigerian-American painter known for large-scale, vibrant geometric abstract paintings that explore color relationships and cultural identity.

**Bridget Riley** (b. 1931)

English painter known for her Op Art paintings that create optical illusions through precise geometric patterns and color relationships.
