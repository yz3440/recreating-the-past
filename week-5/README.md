## Artists

**Ken Knowlton** (1931-2022)

American computer graphics pioneer at Bell Labs. Invented several programming languages for computer animation and collaborated with artists to create ASCII and pixel-based art.

**Lillian Schwartz** (1927-2024)

American artist known for pioneering work in computer-aided art. Created groundbreaking works at Bell Labs combining technology with visual arts.

**Jack Whitten** (1939-2018)

American abstract painter known for innovative techniques and materials, including his "Black Monolith" series that explored mosaic-like arrangements.
