## Topic

## Artists

**Woody and Steina Vasulka**

Pioneering video artists who explored the electronic medium. Woody (1937-2019) and Steina (b. 1940) founded The Kitchen in NYC and created groundbreaking video synthesizer works.

**Rosa Menkman** (b. 1983)

Dutch artist and theorist whose work focuses on visual artifacts created by accidents in digital media, known for her "Glitch Studies Manifesto."
