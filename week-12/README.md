# Week 12

Monday, May 5, 2026 | In person (E15-341)

## Topic

BYOA - Bring Your Own Artist

## Description

This week, students present an artist of their choosing and share a recreation of their work. This is an opportunity to explore artists not covered in the syllabus and expand the collective knowledge of the class.

## Guidelines

1. Choose an artist whose work resonates with you
2. Research their background, techniques, and body of work
3. Recreate one of their works using code
4. Present your findings and recreation to the class

## Homework

Prepare your BYOA presentation and recreation.
